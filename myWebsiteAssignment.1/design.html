Goal alignment for my website:
My goal for this website is to efficiently convey to the users more about me as this is a profile website and its main purpose is for the users to get to know who you are and what you are about. However, I do not want to make it just factual and boring for the user and for myself. I want the user to be able to engage with my website and feel more connected to it through their experience of it but I want them to at the end of their journey know more about me and my website. Regarding this website the target audience/user would be people who want to get to know more about you either because you are starting a new business, or they are looking for people to hire. A website like this one is more about giving a good first impression so that the user may take away the type of character you have as well as the skills and passion you possess. the content of my website is information about me as well as the work that I have done. It is a portfolio that represents me.

Notes on my visual aesthetic: The big idea that I have for my visual aesthetic is a feminine, soft, and romantic one. I would like my visual aesthetic to encapsulate the beauty of all things feminine. However, these design aspects will also be a representation of me. Hence why I am going for pastel pinks, white, gold, and may be cream as well for the colour palette of my website. I also have this engraved image in my mind of adding some sort of design aspect that will make use of flower line drawings. There is something so simple yet so elegant about line drawings especially of flowers. In my opinion the comparison between the feminine and flowers is an accurate one at that. Each flower is different and unique just as each woman is too. There is huge symbolism and meaning behind the visual design choices I am thinking of incorporating into my website. This can make a website special and interesting when there is meaning associated with every little detail that goes into creating it. For the name of my website, I want it to be something quirky and weird in a good way. A name that will stick with the user because it is different and original. I am therefore thinking of creating a name that contains alliteration or some form of a figure of speech. I might also add a logo that displays in its entirety what I am trying to convey with the visual aesthetic of my website. It might be a figure of a woman or a bouquet of flowers.
